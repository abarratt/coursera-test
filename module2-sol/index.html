Choose Your Character

Nero

Really likes eating plastic. When his position is not revealed by the sounds of crinkling plastic, Nero is an excellent stealth type.

Special powers include his soul snatching glare and the destructive powers of his tail.

Atreus

Master of deception. He portrays himself as a love bug when in reality, he is an all consuming beast. Loves food more than life itself.

Special powers include ear-shattering meows and his ragdoll body slam.

Chance

The drive and energy levels of this character are unsurpassed. Be warned that when the chase begins, it will never end until Chance says so.

Special powers include DESTRUCTION and the healing magic of love.
